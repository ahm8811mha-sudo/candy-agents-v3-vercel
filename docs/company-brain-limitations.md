# Company Brain v1 limitations

This release establishes the architecture and controlled runtime for company intelligence. It does not claim autonomous business management without evidence or approvals.

- Predictions are analytical outputs, not guarantees.
- Simulations depend on the completeness and quality of stored facts.
- Government portals that require OTP, payment, CAPTCHA, legal attestation, or final submission remain human checkpoints.
- ZATCA remains sandbox-controlled until certified production credentials and compliance validation are completed.
- Marketplace distribution is disabled; the current skills registry is an internal governed runtime.
- Multi-company isolation is implemented structurally, while the current product surface remains private and single-owner.
- The knowledge graph augments PostgreSQL; it does not replace operational source tables.
